About Me

Hello! I’m Yasha Sharma, a dedicated participant in the Powercoders program, passionate about transitioning into a career in technology. My background is rich with problem-solving and collaboration, skills I now apply rigorously to software development.

I thrive on taking complex problems and breaking them down into elegant, manageable solutions. I have experience in full-stack development concepts, having built projects using Python, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS. I have expanded my toolkit into the world of Cloud Computing and DevOps, focusing on building scalable infrastructure and efficient CI/CD pipelines. I am passionate about the intersection of human logic and machine efficiency, leveraging Prompt Engineering to optimize development and exploring the speed of Low-Code development to deliver rapid, high-impact solutions. I enjoy the process of learning new technologies and immediately applying that knowledge to build functional and user-friendly applications.

This unique background—blending five years of disciplined enterprise history with a fresh, modern approach to AI-automation and Cloud-native tools—gives me a perspective that few developers have. I am a lifelong learner committed to growth and real-world results. I am currently seeking a junior developer position or an internship where I can contribute my growing technical expertise and enthusiastic work ethic to a forward-thinking team. Let’s connect and build something amazing!
